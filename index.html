How It Works

End-to-end trade facilitation

We handle everything between the manufacturer and your door — so you can focus on your customers.

01

Source & Quality Assure

We work exclusively with ISO 9001:2015 certified Pakistani manufacturers, verifying product specifications, certifications and factory standards before any order is placed.

02

Order & Finance

We manage the purchase order, trade documentation and financing coordination — including guidance on letters of credit and trade finance options to ease cash flow.

03

Ship & Clear

We coordinate international freight, handle export documentation from Pakistan, and support your import clearance process into your country.

04

Deliver & Support

We ensure delivery to your specified location and provide ongoing after-sales coordination — warranty support, spare parts sourcing and technical documentation.

05

Grow Together

As our partner, you get access to an expanding product range, preferential pricing on repeat orders, and a dedicated relationship manager.
